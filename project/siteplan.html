Site Name

Heritage

This website is designed to serve as an online platform for Heritage, a trusted company with expertise in buying and selling antiques and jewelry. Heritage provides a wide range of services, including the purchase and sale of valuable antiques and fine jewelry. Additionally, the company offers complimentary evaluations of antiques, helping individuals assess the value of their items. Heritage also specializes in estate clearances, assisting clients with the efficient and respectful handling of personal estates. Whether you're looking to buy, sell, or gain expert insights into your antiques, Heritage is here to help.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a means of reaching an audience we have not yet recieved, an online audience. It offers a place for the use to contact us with out information listed. Along with that it also provides a form the user can fill out that they can ask questions and make requests including giving us thier information so that we can contact them. It also allows the user to view the items we have for sale and to learn about the history and the purpose of the company
