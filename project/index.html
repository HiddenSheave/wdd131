History

Heritage Estate Clearances, established in 1978 and based in the heart of the West Midlands, is a family-run business specializing in house clearances, the buying and selling of antiques, and fine jewelry. With over 40 years of experience, we offer a professional and compassionate service, helping individuals and families manage estate belongings with care. Our expertise in antiques and jewelry ensures fair pricing and valuable insights on unique and historical treasures. As a family business, we pride ourselves on delivering a personalized, trustworthy service to our local community.

Our Purpose

The purpose of Heritage Estate Clearances is simple: to help people through challenging times with dignity and respect. Since we started, we have been specializing in house clearances, antiques, and fine jewelry. We are committed to providing the West Midlands community with a trusted service that offers fair pricing, expert advice, and compassionate care. Whether you’re selling antique treasures or clearing an estate of a friend or family member, our goal is to make the process seamless and stress-free while ensuring the highest level of integrity and professionalism.
